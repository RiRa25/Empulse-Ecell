Meet the Sharks

Krishna Nidavanche

Founder & MD, Naadbramha Group

With 8 years of experience in the food industry, Krishna has been instrumental in shaping Naadbramha Dosa Idli Centre into a trusted brand. Coming from a farming background on the Maharashtra-Karnataka border, his journey began with agriculture and later transitioned into the fruit business in Pune. In 2016, with dedication and teamwork, he transformed a small handcart into a full-fledged food venture, committed to serving fresh, hygienic, and authentic flavors. His vision is to expand Naadbramha to 100 outlets across India by 2025, bringing quality and consistency to every customer.
