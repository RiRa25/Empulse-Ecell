About Empulse

Empulse 2025, the flagship fest of E-Cell Yukta, is set to be an exciting platform for aspiring entrepreneurs. Participants will have unique opportunities to test their business acumen, problem-solving skills, and creativity. The fest will also feature engaging speaker sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities, making it a must-attend for budding entrepreneurs.

About E-Cell Yukta

E-Cell Yukta is a student-run initiative at MKSSS’s Cummins College of Engineering, Pune, supported by the Wadhwani Foundation’s National Entrepreneurship Network. We are dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurial vision and instinct among women engineers. Through various activities such as podcasts, E-summits, speaker sessions, workshops, competitions, and more, we aim to raise awareness about entrepreneurship and provide the necessary guidance for aspiring entrepreneurs.

We offer resources like mentors, consultancy, funding, and networking opportunities with elite CEOs. Regular events include workshops like the E-leader workshop, business model workshops, and sessions such as DSI (Dream, Stimulate, Inspire). Every year, during our Technical Fest, we organize a Business Idea Competition with cash prizes and mentoring from renowned judges and founders, offering selected entrepreneurs support for their startups.
